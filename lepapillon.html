Le Papillon

Group Project Fall 2018
Tags: Interactive Projection
Toolkits: Processing, Kinect

Trapped in forests of cement, people are increasingly disconnected with nature. In the rare moments when people try to get close to nature, they are often left out by its little residents, as they are hurt by human development. Once everywhere around us, butterflies now live only in our childhood memories. Seeking to rebuild the connection between Man & Nature, Le Papillon enables people to interact flocking butterflies with spontaneous body movements. Through creative technology, we invite people go back to their childhood and feel the connection with nature.
