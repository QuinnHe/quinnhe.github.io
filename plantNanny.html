Plant Nanny

Group Project Spring 2017
Tags: Interactive Arts, Digital Farm
Toolkits: Arduino, Processing, Valve

Â An interactive installation that helps people to water plants when they are traveling outside.
Once the plant needs water, the owner will get a message from Plant Nanny on his/her phone. When the owner press a button, the Plant Nanny at home will automatically water the plant, ensuring the soil stay at a specific humidity level.
