The Other

Group Project Spring 2018
Tags: 3D Character, Animation
Toolkits: UE4, Adobe Fuse, Blender, Adobe Premiere

Take inspiration from film Secret Superstar 神秘巨星, the project is an interactive film tells an Indian teenage girl who aspires to be a singer and her romance with an American star.


Plot:

The girl is very passionate about singing, but her father is against her dream of becoming a singer. She records a song wearing a mask, so others can't find out her identity, and uploads the video on YouTube under the name of 'Secret Superstar'. She becomes an overnight sensation and grabs the attention of the American star, who loses himself after becoming famous.

Process:

I. Creating Video Clips
*Characters
Building virtual actors is the most difficult part in our whole project. I try three different softwares to finish the whole process of creating characters.
First, we need to have a skeleton of characters. Though we do have a lot of 3D models on the internet, it's necessary to build our own because of the plot we have. It's really hard to find an Indian girl wearing a mask from the internet(which is free) directly.
A. Have a basic skeleton, including legs, arms, body etc.
B. Clothing the skeleton, including mask, hats, hair etc.
C. Change the texture of clothing, so that it matches the identity of characters.

Previous Next

Second, we have to add armatures so that the character can be imported to unreal properly later. What we do is to send the skeleton from Fuse to Mixamo, download skeleton and then import to Blender.
A. Import the .fbx file and let the armatures be added automatically.
B. Switch to sculpture mode and fill some body parts lost during the import process.


Third, we need to add animation to the characters. Thanks to Mixamo, we can get tons of beautiful animations. What we do is to download animations from Mixamo, and import it to UE4.
A. Choose animation matching our plot on Mixamo and download it.
B. Import the animation .fbx file to Blender and export it directly without any modification.
C. Import the .fbx file to UE4, set the skeleton to the skeleton we already build in the previous steps. We can easily get a good animation character after we drag it to the scene.
*Environment
Compared to creating characters, building environment is quite fast. What we do is download 3D models from the Internet and import it to UE4. After building light, we can easily get a good setting.
